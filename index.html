Welcome to Abby and Rob's Betton Brewhouse!

Nestled in the heart of Tallahassee's Betton Hills neighborhood, Betton Brewhouse is Rob's personal passion project and homebrew hobby. As friends and family, we welcome you into our home and invite you to explore and select a beer to enjoy with us.

What began as a curiosity in 2022 has grown into a true passion for the craft. Every batch is a new opportunity to learn, experiment, and improve. Some of Rob's perfected recipes (and family favorites) can be found on draft in our home. Each beer is brewed with care, shaped by hard-learned lessons and a love for the process with an eye torward enjoyment with you. At home, it's all about good beer and good company: sip, savor, stay awhile.

Styles Brewed

  • 5 SRM Czech Pilsner

    Full bodied lager with noticable hops

    A crisp, golden lager brewed with floor-malted Bohemian Pilsner malt and a generous dose of Saaz hops. Clean and floral with a balanced bitterness, this lager is fermented cold and conditioned long for a smooth, authentic finish.

    • ABV: 5%
    • Fermentation: 4 weeks (cold lagered)
    • Hops: Saaz
    • Grains: Pilsner
    • IBU: 35

  • 6 SRM Golden Ale

    Refreshing dry ale

    Light-bodied and refreshing, this British-style ale features Maris Otter malt and East Kent Goldings hops. It's subtly fruity with a gentle bitterness, making it an easy-drinker on warm days. Fermented using traditional English ale yeast.

    • ABV: 4.3%
    • Fermentation: 6 days
    • Hops: East Kent Goldings, Northern Brewer
    • Grains: Pale and Biscuit Malts
    • IBU: 44

  • 13 SRM Red Ale

    Balanced with a touch of hops

    An Irish-inspired ale with a smooth malt backbone and hints of caramel and toast. A touch of roasted barley adds depth and color, while East Kent Goldings hops provide earthy balance. Uses traditional English ale yeast.

    • ABV: 4.3%
    • Fermentation: 9 days
    • Hops: East Kent Goldings
    • Grains: Pale Malt, Caramel 40L, Roasted Barley
    • IBU: 26

  • 17 SRM Brown Ale

    Nutty, chocolatey, and balanced

    Rich and nutty with layers of chocolate, biscuit, and toffee malt flavors. American hops lend a mild citrus finish, creating a balanced and satisfying pint rooted in British tradition with an American twist. Fermented with American ale yeast.

    • ABV: 5.3%
    • Fermentation: 9 days
    • Hops: Northern Brewer, Cascade
    • Grains: Pale Malt, Caramel 40L, Chocolate Malt
    • IBU: 29
